It was manufactured by Thales Alenia Space and launched on 12 May 2018 from Kennedy Space Center, USA.The project is being implemented by Bangladesh Telecommunication Regulatory Commission (BTRC) in 2008 working hand-in-hand with US-based Space Partnership International, LLC.
